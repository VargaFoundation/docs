Welcome to the Varga Foundation docs. We build and support open-source solutions for data and AI that are accessible, ethical, and community-driven.

## Projects

- [Vorath](./vorath/index.md)
  - HDFS CSI plugin for Kubernetes. Mount HDFS volumes through the standard CSI interface, with Helm charts and operational best practices.

- [Nexberos](./nexberos/index.md)
  - Authentication and federation utilities for modern data/AI platforms. Designed for simple, secure integration.

- [Rivio](./rivio/index.md)
  - Data ingestion and governance toolchain with a strong focus on lineage and accessibility.

- [Weave](./weave/index.md)
  - Modular toolkit to orchestrate data and AI workflows.

Each section includes an overview, installation, configuration, and links to the corresponding repositories and resources.
